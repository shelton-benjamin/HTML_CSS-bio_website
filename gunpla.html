A hobby I recently started is building Gunpla which are model kits based on the famous Gundam anime series. The word Gunpla is a portmanteau of Gundam plastic models. It's quite fun and relaxing to assemble the models and admire what you've created. You just pop the pieces out of the plastic sheets and snap them together.

Here's a chart of the models I currently have and the Gundam series they appeared in.
